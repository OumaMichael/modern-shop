== Description ==

The Poultry Farm Theme is a specialized and versatile website template designed for poultry farming businesses, including egg production farms, broiler farming, organic poultry farming, and free-range poultry farms. It provides dedicated sections for showcasing farm setup, poultry housing, feeding systems, breeding practices, health management, and farm biosecurity to highlight quality and safety standards. This theme helps promote poultry products such as eggs, chicken meat, organic produce, and value-added farm products while also supporting details about vaccination programs, sustainable farming, and disease management. With its modern layout, responsive design, and vibrant imagery, it ensures a professional online presence for poultry farmers, hatcheries, and agricultural service providers. Built with WooCommerce integration for easy product selling and customization, it allows users to display farm equipment, services, and testimonials while connecting effectively with customers. Features like image galleries, service showcases, contact forms, and mobile optimization make the Poultry Farm Theme an excellent choice for agricultural websites, livestock businesses, and farm equipment promotions.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your Poultry Farm right away.
